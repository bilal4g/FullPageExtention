How math is made AI-readable

When you capture a page, FullPage Studio reads the actual equations out of the page's source code (MathML, KaTeX, MathJax and LaTeX are stored right in the HTML on almost every math site). Those exact equations are handed to you as a copy-paste bundle in the Studio's ✨ Math for AI panel. You paste that text together with your screenshot, and the AI reads the real equations instead of guessing from pixels. If a page bakes equations into images with no readable source, use Export at Max 8K so the picture itself is crisp enough to read.
